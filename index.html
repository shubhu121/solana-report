The DePIN Winning Playbook

Successful DePIN projects on Solana follow a proven strategy that combines bootstrapping network effects with a pragmatic approach to long-term sustainability and enterprise integration.

1

Bootstrap with Tokens & Easy Onboarding

Incentivize early contributors with token rewards to build network supply. Lower the barrier to entry by supporting widely available hardware (e.g., smartphones).

2

Achieve Real-World Utility

Focus on providing a service with genuine, existing demand (e.g., wireless, mapping, compute) and offer competitive pricing to attract non-crypto native customers.

3

Transition to Enterprise Revenue

Develop enterprise-friendly interfaces and sales teams to secure stable, long-term revenue from corporate clients, ensuring sustainability beyond token inflation.

4

Foster Ecosystem Composability

Collaborate with other Solana protocols. Combine location data with compute, or wireless with storage, to create novel, high-value services that are greater than the sum of their parts.
